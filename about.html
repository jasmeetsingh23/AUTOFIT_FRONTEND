// About Us //

AutoFit Is A Brand You Can Count On.

Dewan Chand Panna Lal (DCPL) is an ISO 9001:2015 accredited enterprise established since 1951. Starting from a small trading shop in the heart of Old Delhi, Chawri Bazar Chandni Chowk, all the members of DCPL have always worked hard towards satisfaction of their customers by providing best service and quality hand tools. AUTOFIT was introduced in 2014 by Mr. Sanjay Kr. Gupta with a small range of hand tools consisting of ¼ “ and ½” sq. dr. sockets imported from China and Taiwan. Over the years we all have worked hard to give best quality products and maximum range of hand tools to our channel partners.

SINCE 1951

With the heavenly blessings of his ancestors along with great grandfather Sh. PANNA LAL AGARWAL, grandfather Sh. HARI RAM AGARWAL and father Sh. SANJAY Kr. GUPTA, today the business is run by family of MR. SAHIL GUPTA, whose passion and hard work has made AUTOFIT provide our customers a huge variety of top-quality hand tools.

QUALITY HAND TOOLS

AUTOFIT HAND TOOLS stand by the quality of its tools, ensuring satisfaction with every purchase. We are one of the leading brands for ‘SOCKETS & ACCESSORIES’ in India as of 2024. This has been possible because of never compromising quality, best prices, continuous availability and increasing product ranges. Our product catalogue has diverse types of Hand tools equipment which not only keeps loyal customers within the brand but also attracts new ones seeking different features or price points. We have worked hard to build our excellent reputation and are always dedicated to providing the best to our clients. Designed with ergonomics in mind, we provide hand tool kits with superior comfort and control, reducing fatigue and enhancing productivity.
